Here I have put together a selection of restaurants I love to eat at whenever I go back home. Eating in Hong Kong for vegetarians can seem difficult at first, but if you know where to go, you'll have no problems at all!

Important Note: Hong Kong is currently the hottest place for mock-meat products, so there are plenty of innovative dishes to try. However, if you don't enjoy meat-like flavours and/or textures, this might be something to watch out for.
